Welcome to Snake Water Gun game!
At the end of match result will be publish.
Follow the given rule to win this match.

Rules :>
rule 1 : This game for only two people (Computer vs You)
rule 2 : There r total 10 round in this game
rule 3 : There r total 3 bets Snake,Water,Gun
rule 4 : You can choose only 1 bet

How to win :
choose any bet and comparing with Computer bet You will win/loose.
example - Snake vs Gun > Gun win, Snake vs Water > Snake win

Comparing Method:> if you choose:
Snake > then 'Snake' can Beat 'Water' by drinking it but can be beaten by 'Gun'
Water > then 'Water' can beat 'Gun' by damaging it but can be beaten by 'Snake'
Gun   > then 'Gun' can beat 'Snake' by killing it but can be beaten by 'Water'
